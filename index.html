I designed various experiences and helped businesses to engage with their customers both physically and digitally, from magazines, events, apps, face-to-face interaction…etc.

Experience

2013 Worked in the lifestyle magazine and created stories for brands, such as Perrier, Samova, Wang De Chuan (Chinese fine tea since 1862)…etc. 2015 worked in Davines, a sustainable haircare brand, in charge of integrated marketing, campaigns, and events. 2017 moved to Denmark and studied Service Design at Aalborg University. Since 2019, started to work as a freelance UX & service designer, and in my second identity, to collaborate with Nihao Yao (Copenhagen top 12 restaurants, selected by Timeout) to provide fresh and authentic Taiwanese food to Copenhageners.
